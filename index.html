About Me

Hey everyone! I'm Timéo, a French student currently enrolled at Cégep de Vanier. I'm passionate about music, particularly drumming, and I'm also deeply interested in photography and video creation.

Photography allows me to capture unique moments, while video creation excites me because it can tell compelling stories. I value minimalism in design, as I believe it's crucial for effective communication. I'm also keen to explore web development, given its importance in today's digital age.

In summary, I'm Timéo, an enthusiastic student with a love for music, photography, and video, along with a strong interest in minimalist design and web development. I approach design with an open mind and value collective efforts, consistency, and creativity.
